What is SEAMS School 2025 Baguio?

Inverse problems and optimal control have garnered significant attention recently, particularly in applied mathematics and engineering. This school aims to bring together experts in these fields, emphasizing both theoretical foundations and recent advancements in the fields. Participants will benefit from introductory lectures on cutting-edge numerical techniques for tackling various inverse and optimal control problems. The program features lecture series and hands-on computational exercises, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of both theoretical and numerical aspects.
